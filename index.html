Get the Star Power!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four stars to choose from. By clicking on a star you will add a certain amount of Star Points to your current total.

You win the game by matching your total amount of Star Points to the random number of Star Points needed. You lose the game if your total amount of Star Points exceeds the random needed amount.

During each game the amount of Star Points each star is worth is hidden though it stays the same per game, only changing after each new game is started.
